About

Making learning free, practical, and accessible

My name is Arman Hasan. I am a 10th class student from a middle-class family. This website is a single-handed project built by me — not by a company or team. My purpose is to provide free, useful skills and resources to students and learners so they can improve without paying for costly courses.

Who built this

Built single-handedly by Arman Hasan — a motivated student committed to open access learning.

Purpose

My goal is to provide free skills and resources — to help learners improve typing, basic coding, and practical digital skills that are useful in studies and future jobs. I design lessons and practice tools that are simple, step-by-step, and focused on real improvement.

My story

I come from a modest background and have faced limitations in resources and guidance. Rather than accept those limits, I turned my learning into a project to help others. This website is the result of long hours of learning, testing and building — to share everything I found useful with fellow students.

Vision & commitment

I regularly update this website with new lessons, features, and improvements so that it stays practical and easy to use. With time and support, I want this platform to become a reliable learning hub where students can build skills step-by-step, without stress.

Get in touch

I welcome feedback, suggestions, and support — every message helps me improve the site and make learning easier for more students.
